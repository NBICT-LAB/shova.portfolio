About me

I'm Sadhan Verma

I founded North Bengal Information and Communication Technology Laboratory NBICT LAB at Dinajpur in 2018. I'm also the founder of coursebangla.com.
I have been running and directing an ICT training project for students and teachers at Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University, Dinajpur, Bangladesh since 2017. I have five years of experience in the ICT training profession as a trainer. I'm a popular online instructor at www.youtube.com/c/nbictlab. I'm a dedicated developer at Rangpur Online School rangpuronlineschool.com. I trains up the district ambassadors of a2i and ICT4E, who are the teachers from many different schools and colleges of the Rangpur division.
I have completed the Bachelor of Science program from the faculty of Fisheries, Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University in 2016.
